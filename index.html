Our Services

Aesthetic and Rejuvenation

We offer PRP therapy, microneedling, skin rejuvenation, and more to help you look and feel your best.

Our aesthetic services are designed to boost your confidence with personalized treatment plans. We offer:

  • PRP Therapy (Platelet-Rich Plasma): Used for both skin rejuvenation and hair restoration, helping to stimulate collagen production and cell regeneration.
  • Microneedling: Promotes collagen and elastin production by creating controlled micro-injuries on the skin, helping reduce acne scars, stretch marks, and fine lines.
  • Glutathione Therapy: An intravenous antioxidant treatment for detoxification and skin brightening, helping achieve an even skin tone.
  • IV Vitamin Therapy: Customized vitamin cocktails administered via IV to promote overall skin health, hydration, and a glowing skin tone.
  • Anti-Aging Treatments: Collagen boosters and wrinkle reduction treatments such as botox to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Scar Revision and Keloid Treatments: Specialized scar treatments to reduce scar size and improve appearance using corticosteroid injections, laser therapy, and silicone sheets.
  • Facial Contouring: Non-surgical options to contour facial features, such as dermal fillers for lips, cheeks, and jawline to enhance facial symmetry.

Diabetes Care

Comprehensive diabetes management, monitoring, dietary plans, and foot care services.

Our diabetes care program includes:

  • Blood Glucose Monitoring: Regular monitoring to keep your blood sugar levels in check.
  • Individualized Dietary Planning: Customized meal plans to help manage diabetes effectively.
  • Foot Care: Prevention and management of diabetes-related foot complications through regular check-ups and specialized care.
  • Patient Education: Providing tools and knowledge to help patients live a healthier lifestyle and manage their condition independently.
  • Medication Management: Ensuring that patients take their diabetes medication correctly and safely.

General Outpatient

Routine check-ups, preventive healthcare, chronic disease management, and prescription services.

Our outpatient services include:

  • Routine Check-Ups: Regular health assessments to catch potential issues early.
  • Preventive Healthcare: Immunizations, screenings, and lifestyle advice to maintain your health.
  • Chronic Disease Management: Comprehensive care for chronic conditions such as hypertension, asthma, and arthritis.
  • Prescription Support: Timely prescriptions and medication management to ensure continuity of care.
  • Minor Procedures: On-site minor surgical procedures such as wound suturing, incision, and drainage.

Wound Care

Expert care for acute and chronic wounds, including dressing changes, infection management, and debridement.

We provide specialized wound care services including:

  • Acute and Chronic Wounds: Treatment for wounds that require ongoing care.
  • Dressing Changes: Proper and timely dressing changes to promote healing and prevent infections.
  • Infection Management: Monitoring and treating wound infections to accelerate recovery.
  • Advanced Wound Healing Therapies: Techniques like debridement and hyperbaric oxygen therapy to facilitate quicker healing.
  • Pressure Ulcer Prevention: Preventive care to reduce the risk of developing pressure sores, especially in bedridden patients.

Home Care

Home-based services, elderly care, and rehabilitation support tailored to your needs.

Our home care services include:

  • Nursing Care: Skilled nurses provide medical assistance at home for recovery and support.
  • Elderly Care: Assistance with daily activities for seniors to help maintain independence.
  • Rehabilitation Support: Physical and occupational therapy to help regain strength and mobility.
  • Daily Living Assistance: Help with grooming, bathing, meal preparation, and medication reminders.
  • Palliative Care: Compassionate care for individuals with serious illnesses, focused on improving quality of life through pain management and emotional support.

Specialist Consultations

Consult with our specialists in cardiology, endocrinology, pediatrics, and more.

Our specialists offer consultations in the following areas:

  • Cardiology: Diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions, including hypertension, arrhythmia, and coronary artery disease.
  • Endocrinology: Expert care for hormonal disorders such as diabetes, thyroid problems, and adrenal gland issues.
  • Pediatrics: Comprehensive care for infants, children, and adolescents, including immunizations, growth monitoring, and developmental assessments.
  • Neurology: Evaluation and treatment of neurological conditions, including epilepsy, migraines, and neurodegenerative disorders.
  • Dermatology: Diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail conditions, including acne, eczema, and psoriasis.

Medical Tourism

Health packages for international patients, pre- and post-treatment care, and accommodation assistance.

We offer comprehensive packages for international patients, including:

  • Pre-Treatment Consultation: Virtual or in-person consultations to prepare patients for procedures and assess medical needs.
  • Post-Treatment Care: Follow-up services to ensure successful recovery after medical procedures, including physical therapy and medication management.
  • Accommodation Assistance: Assistance with travel and accommodation arrangements for patients and their families, ensuring comfort and convenience.
  • Medical Concierge Services: Dedicated assistance throughout the treatment journey, including translation services and local support.
  • Health Packages: Tailored healthcare packages that include transportation, accommodation, and treatment services to ensure a seamless experience.
